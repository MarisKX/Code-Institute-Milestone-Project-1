What we are?

Zandbergen Transport - founded in 1939 - is a family business specializing in the transport of beverages, food and building materials. The third generation of the Zandbergen family is currently active. The locations of Zandbergen Transport can be found throughout the Netherlands. In this way we can offer an optimal total solution to our clients. After its foundation in 1939, Zandbergen Transport focused its services on the transport of straw, fertilizer and flower bulbs. Meanwhile, the course is adapted to the needs of today's market, namely the transport of food, beverages and construction materials. Thanks to automation, employee training, innovative communication equipment, on-board computers and the branches at various locations, Zandbergen Transport has been able to put itself on the map again and again.
