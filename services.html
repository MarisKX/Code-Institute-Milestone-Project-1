Transport that requires different temperature regimes


Hygiene, flexibility and reliability are the three spearheads that are central to Zandbergen Transport's refrigerated and frozen transport. We specialize in the transportation of a variety of refrigerated and frozen products to distribution centers and supermarket branches. Since transport usually consists of food, we attach great importance to appropriate quality certificates. We also provide clean, well-maintained and new transport equipment. It is with reason that Zandbergen Transport has been providing transport for clients such as Albert Heijn since the 1950s. In order to provide this supermarket chain with an adequate service, Zandbergen Transport has taken place internally at the Albert Heijn office.

Tautliner transport


Zandbergen's tautliner transport is used for a wide variety of freight for different clients. For example, we provide transport between suppliers and retailers throughout the Netherlands and Europe. We also drive freight to and from distribution centers for large clients. The tautliners are equipped with closed trailers, so that the cargo is transported free of moisture, dirt and dust. Since our tautliners are continuously active nationwide, we can respond flexibly to the local and regional wishes of our clients. The coil trailers from Zandbergen Transport are flat trailers with a steel gutter. In the bottom of the trailer, a hatch can be opened to place a roll of steel on the underlying beam. This roll usually weighs around 20 tons and is therefore loaded into the car by crane and unloaded in the same way. Tata Steel is one of our clients, for which the well-maintained coil trailers are used.

Automatic loading and unloading system

Zandbergen Transport's automatic loading and unloading system consists of a chain conveyor in the trailer, which can be connected to a stationary installation within the loading and unloading dock of the distribution center. Two pallets are placed on the chain conveyor, after which the conveyor continues to run. This is repeated until the track is completely full. The pallets then slide out of the trailer on the belt. For the transport of soft drinks, among other things, Zandbergen Transport has 25 semi-trailers with the so-called self-unloading loading and unloading floors. The driver places the trailer against the loading and unloading dock; in just a few minutes the trailer is then automatically loaded or unloaded.
